Retaining Wall Tips

Proper Base Preparation

A well-compacted base is crucial for wall stability. Excavate to the required depth and use crushed stone for proper drainage.

Drainage Considerations

Always include drainage behind retaining walls to prevent water pressure buildup. Use perforated pipe and gravel backfill.

Wall Height Limits

For walls over 3-4 feet, consider consulting an engineer. Taller walls may require special design considerations.

Local Regulations

Check with your local building department for permits and regulations regarding retaining wall construction.
